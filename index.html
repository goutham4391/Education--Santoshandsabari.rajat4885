Why Santhosh & Sabari IAS ?

We know that learning is easier when you have excellent teachers. Our instructors are passionate about the subjects they teach and bring enthusiasm into their seminars and courses. Our educators have achieved an advanced qualification in their field like Ph.D/ NET/ JRF. Moreover, they themselves have appeared in various competitive Exams and have reached a height.

Apart from that, we run only one batch at a time for any of the courses we provide and those batches are small batches consisting of around 50 students. So, there is no crowd in our batches and so we never lose attention on students. It means, we individually focus on each student and continuously monitor and mentor their progress.

More than that, we spend a lot of time for each batch, for example, we conduct UPSC Civil service Preliminary cum Main GS batch for 11 months. It means around 1300 hours of class room training in 11 months.

Most importantly, our fees structure is the lowest and most affordable among the institutes. We also provide term based payment of fees so as to ease the stress of the parents.

What we offer ?

We offer courses for following exams.

  1. UPSC Civil Services Exam which includes IAS/IPS/IRS etc

  2. Indian Forest Service Exam (IFS)

  3. Central Armed Police Force (CAPF) Assistant Commandant Exam

  4. Indian Engineering Service (IES) General Studies Paper

  5. TNPSC Group 1 Exam

  6. TNPSC Assistant Engineers Exam

All of our courses are customized for your needs to make sure you leave with relevant skills to face the exam.

Our Teaching methods

Our method of teaching is very traditional. It is also very comprehensive. It includes

  1. Explanation & Dictation

  2. Complete analysis of Previous year questions every day

  3. Regular tests

We first introduce a concept, explain about it and dictate the notes. We provide hundred percent class notes for all the subjects. It is mandatory to take written notes. We explain to the root level that help the students to learn how to approach and solve a question. The main advantage of hand written class notes is that we can provide the latest updates in each topic.

After every small topic is completed, we present the previous year questions that were asked in various competitive exams. The students must write the questions along with the class notes ( Yes, you read it right).

We conduct tests regularly so that we fine tune them towards their exams.
